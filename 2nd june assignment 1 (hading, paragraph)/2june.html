Biography of A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Name

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Early Life

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
He came from a humble background and worked hard to support his education. His early interest in science and engineering paved the way for a bright future.

Achievements

Dr. Kalam played a key role in India's missile development and space programs. He was known as the "Missile Man of India".
He later served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
He inspired millions of students with his vision for a developed India and a passion for learning.
